About Me

Hello! I'm KEFEI LI, also known as BELQIS. I'm a passionate designer with expertise in graphic design, 3D modeling, and UI/UX design. With a keen eye for detail and a love for creative problem-solving, I strive to create visually stunning and functional designs that leave a lasting impression.
