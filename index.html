About Me

Hello! I'm KEFEI LI, a passionate designer with a strong background in graphic design, 3D modeling, and UI/UX design. With a keen eye for detail and a love for creative problem-solving, I strive to create visually striking and functional designs that leave a lasting impression.

My journey in design has equipped me with a broad skill set, including proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, 3D modeling software, and various UI design tools. Over the years, I've provided design solutions for clients across the internet, pharmaceutical, and automotive industries, working with renowned brands like Tencent, Jaguar Land Rover, Chevrolet, Mercedes-Benz, and Honda. My work has taken me overseas, where I've led design teams on projects ranging from commercial real estate to fashion branding and seasonal promotions.

Beyond design, I have a wide range of hobbies, and am fluent in English, also I speak Turkish and Japanese. I'm continually looking to push the boundaries of different fields. One of my specialties is veterinary medicine, and I hope to find opportunities related to medicine or pets.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring art exhibitions, experimenting with photography, or learning about the latest trends in technology and design.
